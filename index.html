The work spans agentic claims systems with verifiable handoffs, RAG over regulated documents, RLVR pipelines that took a workflow from 78 to 94 percent accuracy, and on-prem inference on bare-metal A100 for buyers who cannot send data over the wire.

The throughline is the same: I am the person who sits between the customer's hardest problem and the engineering team that has to solve it.

Comfortable in front of CIOs and CTOs. Comfortable in the architecture room. Comfortable at the keyboard. cost-latency-privacy trade-offs, model selection, eval design, agentic orchestration patterns. All of it is the conversation I have already been having every week.
